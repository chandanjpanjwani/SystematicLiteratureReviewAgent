It has made life easier for them and their customers, bringing efficiency to all levels of the company and collecting data, product quality, and predictability to all sectors of life. the study of digital technology has not only the background of interdisciplinary integration but also the characteristic of strong practicality. In this paper, the various elements that constitute the digital representation technology were comprehensively analyzed first, and then the design based on the digital technology was compared with the traditional design, which embodied the advantages of digital display technology. Then, combined with the actual design case of Xiangshan Hotel, The corridor design scheme of Xiangshan Hotel was analyzed, and a 3D effect map was designed and applied in detail by using digital technology. Finally, the advantages and disadvantages of digital technology in interior design were verified by comparing and analyzing the design scheme of digital technology with the traditional design scheme.
